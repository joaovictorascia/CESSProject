File Storage Pricing

  • Free accounts:

    • Files start with 8-day lifespan
    • Each download adds 2 days (maximum 60 days)
    • Files become permanent with 30+ downloads every 60 days

  • Premium accounts:

    • Full access requires 50 CESS Tokens
    • Permanent storage: 5 CESS Tokens / GB / Month
    • After reaching subscribed limits, files revert to Free account rules
    • Priority support and advanced features

  • Non-profit organizations:

    • Free permanent storage available for verified non-profit websites and organizations
